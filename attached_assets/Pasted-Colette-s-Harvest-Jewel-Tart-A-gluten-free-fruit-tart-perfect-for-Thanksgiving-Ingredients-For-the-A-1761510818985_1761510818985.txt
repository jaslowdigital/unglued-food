Colette's Harvest Jewel Tart
A gluten-free fruit tart perfect for Thanksgiving
Ingredients
For the Almond Crust:

2 cups almond flour
¼ cup coconut flour
¼ cup maple syrup or honey
6 tablespoons melted butter or coconut oil
1 egg
½ teaspoon vanilla extract
¼ teaspoon salt

For the Vanilla Cream Filling:

8 oz cream cheese, softened
½ cup heavy cream
⅓ cup powdered sugar (ensure it's gluten-free)
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
Zest of 1 lemon

For the Fruit Topping:

2 cups fresh berries (raspberries, blackberries, blueberries)
2 fresh figs, quartered
1 cup red grapes, halved
2 kiwis, peeled and sliced
½ cup pomegranate arils
3 tablespoons apricot jam (for glaze)
1 tablespoon water

Instructions
Make the Crust:

Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9-inch tart pan with removable bottom.
In a large bowl, combine almond flour, coconut flour, and salt.
In a separate bowl, whisk together melted butter, maple syrup, egg, and vanilla.
Pour wet ingredients into dry and mix until a dough forms.
Press dough evenly into the tart pan, going up the sides.
Prick the bottom with a fork and bake for 15-18 minutes until golden.
Cool completely before filling.

Make the Filling:

Beat cream cheese until smooth and fluffy.
Add powdered sugar, vanilla, and lemon zest. Mix well.
In a separate bowl, whip heavy cream to stiff peaks.
Gently fold whipped cream into cream cheese mixture.
Spread filling evenly over cooled crust.
Refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

Arrange the Fruit:

Pat fruit dry with paper towels.
Arrange fruit in concentric circles or artistic pattern over the filling.
In a small saucepan, warm apricot jam with water until liquid.
Brush glaze gently over fruit for a beautiful shine.
Refrigerate until ready to serve.

Tips:

Make the crust and filling the day before; add fruit the day of serving for best results
Choose firm, ripe fruit that's not too juicy
Use autumn fruits like persimmons, pears, or fresh cranberries for a seasonal twist
The tart can be refrigerated for up to 2 days

Serves: 8-10
Prep Time: 30 minutes
Bake Time: 18 minutes
Chill Time: 1 hour
